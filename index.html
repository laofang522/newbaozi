Monster Survivors

A thrilling survival action game where you fight endless waves of monsters with unique characters and powerful abilities.

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense action survival game that challenges players to survive as long as possible against relentless waves of monsters. Choose from multiple unique characters, each with their own special abilities and upgrade paths, and battle your way through increasingly difficult hordes of enemies.

As you progress, you'll collect experience to level up, unlock powerful new abilities, and find items to enhance your character's strength. Every run is different, offering endless replayability and strategic depth.

Game Features

  • Multiple playable characters with unique abilities
  • Roguelite progression with permanent upgrades
  • Hundreds of different enemy types and boss monsters
  • Procedurally generated environments for endless variety
  • Unlock new weapons, abilities, and passive upgrades
  • Beautiful pixel art graphics and atmospheric soundtrack
  • Leaderboards to compete with players worldwide

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies. Collect experience gems to level up and choose new abilities or upgrades. Survive as long as possible while facing increasingly difficult waves of monsters.

The longer you survive, the stronger you'll become, but the enemies will also grow in number and power. Develop your strategy, choose the right upgrades for your playstyle, and see how long you can last in this challenging survival experience!

System Requirements

Monster Survivors is an in-browser game that works on most modern devices. For the best experience, we recommend using an updated browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. The game is fully optimized for both desktop and mobile play.
